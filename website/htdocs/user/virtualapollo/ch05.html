Chapter Scenarios

The following scenarios are included in Virtual Apollo. You will find them in the "Virtual Apollo" folder of the Orbiter Launchpad.

AS-506 Apollo 11 Launch Flight AS-506 - Apollo 11 scenario with launch position at Cape Canaveral using +72.058° launch azimuth and 600s to launch.

AS-510 Apollo 15 Launch Flight AS-510 - Apollo 15 scenario with launch position at Cape Canaveral using +80.008° launch azimuth and 600s to launch. Use this scenario to do the procedures mentioned in the Operational Procedures.

AS-510 Apollo 15 Pre-Launch Flight AS-510 - Apollo 15 scenario with launch position at Cape Canaveral using +80.008° launch azimuth and 1 day to launch. This scenario is intended for tests on the launch pad.

AS-511 Apollo 16 Launch Flight AS-511 - Apollo 16 scenario with launch position at Cape Canaveral using +72.034° launch azimuth and 600s to launch.

AS-512 Apollo 17 Launch Flight AS-512 - Apollo 17 scenario with launch position at Cape Canaveral using +91.503° launch azimuth and 600s to launch.

Launch position North Pole Test scenario with start position at +90° latitude, 0° longitude, 0° heading (0° azimuth) and 600s to launch.

Launch position Zero Test scenario with start position at 0° latitude, 0° longitude, 0° heading (0° azimuth) and 600s to launch.

No Crawler Flight AS-506 - Apollo 11 test scenario without the crawler with start position at Cape Canaveral (Lat. +28.5208°), +72.058° launch azimuth and 600s to launch.
