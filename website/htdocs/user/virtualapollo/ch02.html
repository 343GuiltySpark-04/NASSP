Saturn V

The Saturn V is the Apollo launch vehice and has three stages called SIC, SII and SIVB. The Saturn has a on-board computer of it's own and can do all necessary guidance, navigation and control tasks by itself. In Virtual Apollo the Saturn is totally controlled by the NASSP autopilot. So there are no controls for the Saturn and no interaction between the AGC and the Saturn at the moment.
